About the Program

An initiative by Selangor Govenrment & LPHS forresidential buildings to upgrade buildings' LED or traditional lights to achieve enegry savings up to 81% and create a sustainable living.

Download Endorsement Certificate

* Click City Council logo to download their individual letters of endorsement.

With endorsement from the 12 City Councils in the state of Selangor, the program aims to benefit the Joint Management Bodies (JMB)s and Management Corporations (MC)s of strata bui;dings within the state with energy-efficient lighting at no upfront cost to the building management.
